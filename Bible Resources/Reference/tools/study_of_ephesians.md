## Series Overview

Ephesians divides neatly into two halves:

| Section | Chapters | Theme | Key Verse |
|---------|----------|-------|-----------|
| **Doctrinal** | 1–3 | Our Riches in Christ — what God has done | Eph 1:3 |
| **Practical** | 4–6 | Our Walk in Christ — how we live because of it | Eph 4:1 |

The "therefore" in Eph 4:1 and Eph 5:1 marks the pivot from doctrine to duty — but duty is always *grounded in* the doctrine that precedes it.

- **Redeemed** through Christ's blood (v. 7)

## Ephesians 3: The Mystery Revealed

### Key Themes
- **The mystery of Christ** — Gentiles are fellow heirs (vv. 1–6)
- **Paul's stewardship** of the gospel to the Gentiles (vv. 7–13)
- **Paul's prayer for strength** — to know the love of Christ (vv. 14–21)
- **God's power** — able to do far more abundantly than we ask (vv. 20–21)

### Outline (Expositor's Commentary)
1. **The Revelation of the Mystery** (vv. 1–7)
   - Paul the prisoner (v. 1)
   - The mystery made known (vv. 2–6)
   - Paul the minister (vv. 7)
2. **The Proclamation of the Mystery** (vv. 8–13)
   - To the Gentiles (vv. 8–9)
   - To the rulers in heavenly places (v. 10)
   - The boldness of access (vv. 11–13)
3. **The Prayer for Fullness** (vv. 14–21)
   - The Father as source (vv. 14–15)

## Ephesians 5: Walk in Love and Light

### Key Themes
- **Imitate God** as beloved children (vv. 1–2)
- **Walk in love** as Christ loved us (v. 2)

- **Walk as children of light** — identity transformation (vv. 8–10)
- **Expose darkness** — light's mission (vv. 11–14)
- **Walk wisely** — making the most of the time (vv. 15–21)

1. **Love in the Family** (vv. 1–2)

## Cross-Chapter Themes

### Identity → Behavior
Every command in Ephesians flows from identity:
- Chosen → live chosen
- Adopted → live like children
- Light → walk as light
- Beloved → love like the Father

### The "Walk" Series (Ephesians 4–6)
| Walk | Reference | Theme |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Walk worthy | 4:1 | Of your calling |
| Walk differently | 4:17 | Not like the Gentiles |
| Walk in love | 5:2 | As Christ loved |
| Walk as light | 5:8 | Children of light |
| Walk carefully | 5:15 | Making the most of time |

### The Three "Therefore" Points
1. **Eph 4:1** — Doctrine → duty
2. **Eph 5:1** — Identity → imitation
3. **Eph 5:7** (implied) — Light → walk as light

## Quick Reference: Ephesians Book Data

- **Author:** The Apostle Paul
- **Date:** ~AD 60–62 (during Roman imprisonment)
- **Recipients:** The church in Ephesus (and likely a circular letter)
- **Purpose:** To ground believers in their spiritual identity and call them to live consistently with their position in Christ
- **Key Word:** "In Christ" (occurs ~36 times)
- **Key Concept:** The church as the body of Christ — one new humanity
