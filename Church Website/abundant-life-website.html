Who We Are

A church family carrying the gospel to real people.

Abundant Life Family Worship Center exists to glorify God by proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ, gathering and shepherding a regenerate church, and making disciples across Carroll County through the ordinary means of grace.

We are not a perfect church. We are a family of sinners saved by grace, learning to walk together in repentance, truth, love, worship, service, and mission.

Pastor Jason Tilley

Jason serves as senior pastor after previously serving as youth pastor and associate pastor. His burden is faithful exposition, biblical fidelity, Christ-centered preaching, and practical pastoral care.

Know Christ. Join the Church. Grow in Grace. Serve and Send.

What We Believe

Scripture is our final authority.

Our doctrine is centered on the gospel: Christ’s life, death, resurrection, and reign. We want to be clear where Scripture is clear, careful where faithful Christians have differed, and united around the mission Christ has given His church.

Ask a Question

Scripture

The Bible is inspired by God, true in all that it teaches, and sufficient for faith and godliness.

Jesus Christ

Jesus is fully God and fully man. He lived without sin, died for sinners, rose bodily, and reigns as Lord.

Salvation

Salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone. The gospel calls every person to repent and believe.

The Church

The local church is a covenant community of baptized believers under Christ’s Word.

Abundant Life 101

Membership gives clarity to love.

Membership is not a consumer relationship. It is a covenant commitment to follow Christ together as a local church family.

1. Attend Abundant Life 101

Learn the gospel we preach, the doctrine we teach, the mission we share, and the commitment involved in membership.

2. Membership Conversation

Share your testimony, baptism history, questions, and areas where you desire shepherding or service.

3. Covenant Commitment

Walk together in worship, holiness, service, reconciliation, giving, and mission.
